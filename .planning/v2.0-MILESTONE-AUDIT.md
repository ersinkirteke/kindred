### AUTH-06: Sub-90s onboarding flow (Partial)

- **Status:** Partial
- **Phase:** 8 (Authentication & Onboarding)
- **Evidence:** OnboardingReducer and 4-step onboarding views (sign-in, dietary, location, voice teaser) exist in AuthFeature package. However, the integration checker found OnboardingReducer is not `@Presents` in AppReducer, meaning the onboarding carousel may not be presented to new users after first sign-in.
- **Action needed:** Verify onboarding presentation path. If missing, wire OnboardingReducer into AppReducer with `@Presents` and present after first successful sign-in.
