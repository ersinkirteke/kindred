**Create Kindred/docs/what-to-test.md:**
Write a friendly, clear beta tester guide. This text will be pasted into TestFlight's "What to Test" field and shared with testers. Include:

   - **Flow 1: Onboarding** — Open app → complete dietary preferences → select location → skip or complete voice teaser
   - **Flow 2: Browse Recipe Feed** — Scroll feed → view recipe cards → check location-based sorting → try pull-to-refresh
   - **Flow 3: Open Recipe Detail** — Tap recipe card → view ingredients + steps → check hero image loading
   - **Flow 4: Voice Narration** — On recipe detail → tap Play button → listen to voice narration → test pause/skip → check mini player when navigating away
   - **Flow 5: Pantry Scan** — Go to Pantry tab → tap camera icon → scan fridge/receipt → verify items detected → see match % on recipe cards
   - **Flow 6: Subscribe** — Go to Settings → tap subscription → purchase Kindred Pro (sandbox mode) → verify ads removed + unlimited features

3. **How to Report Issues**: Shake device to send feedback via TestFlight (built-in). Include: what you were doing, what happened, what you expected.

4. **Known Limitations**: Sandbox subscriptions may behave differently from production. Voice profiles require audio upload which needs microphone/file access.

5. **Thank You**: Brief appreciation note.
