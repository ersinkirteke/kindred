    - DependencyValues extension with `adClient`
    - NOTE: Actual ad loading happens in the UIViewRepresentable views (AdCardView, BannerAdView) using GADAdLoader delegates — NOT in AdClient. AdClient handles SDK initialization and first-launch suppression only.
