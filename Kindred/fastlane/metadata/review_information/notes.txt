APP DESCRIPTION

Kindred is a cooking app that lets users hear recipes narrated in the voices of their loved ones using AI voice cloning. The app transforms the cooking experience by combining personalized voice narration with recipe discovery, smart pantry scanning, and location-based content.

AI TECHNOLOGY DISCLOSURE

Voice cloning is powered by ElevenLabs AI technology. Users must explicitly consent before uploading any voice audio. The consent screen names ElevenLabs by name and explains how voice data is processed. Users can delete their voice profile at any time from the Settings tab. Voice audio data is processed securely by ElevenLabs and stored in compliance with voice privacy regulations.
