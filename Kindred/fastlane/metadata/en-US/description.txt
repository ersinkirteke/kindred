ABOUT AI VOICE TECHNOLOGY
Kindred uses ElevenLabs, a leading AI voice technology platform, to create voice profiles. Here's what you should know:

• Voice data is only collected with your explicit consent before each upload
• You maintain full control—voice profiles can be deleted from Settings at any time
• Voice recordings are processed securely and used solely for creating your personalized narration experience
• We're transparent about our AI usage and comply with all applicable voice privacy regulations
