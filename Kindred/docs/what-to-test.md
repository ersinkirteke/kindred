# What to Test — Kindred Beta

Welcome to Kindred beta! We'd love your feedback on these core features. Your insights will help us create the best possible cooking experience before our official launch.

## Core Test Flows

Please try each of these flows and let us know what works well and what could be improved.

### Flow 1: Onboarding

**Steps:**
1. Open the app for the first time
2. Complete dietary preferences screen (select any preferences or skip)
3. Select your location (use "My Location" or pick a city manually)
4. Review the voice feature teaser (you can skip or explore further)

**What to look for:** Is the onboarding flow clear and welcoming? Does location detection work properly? Are the instructions easy to understand?

---

### Flow 2: Browse Recipe Feed

**Steps:**
1. From the main Feed tab, scroll through recipe cards
2. Check that recipes appear to be relevant to your location
3. Pull down to refresh the feed
4. Notice the hero images and recipe metadata (cook time, servings, etc.)

**What to look for:** Do the recipes look appealing? Is the feed smooth to scroll? Are the recipe cards easy to read?

---

### Flow 3: Open Recipe Detail

**Steps:**
1. Tap any recipe card from the feed
2. View the full recipe detail screen with hero image
3. Scroll through the ingredients list
4. Scroll through the cooking steps

**What to look for:** Is all the recipe information easy to read? Do images load quickly? Is anything missing that you'd expect to see?

---

### Flow 4: Voice Narration

**Steps:**
1. Open any recipe detail screen
2. Tap the Play button to start voice narration
3. Listen as the AI voice reads the recipe steps aloud
4. Test the pause button while narration is playing
5. Try skipping forward or backward through the recipe steps
6. Navigate to another screen — the mini player should appear at the bottom
7. Tap the mini player to return to the expanded player view

**What to look for:** Does the voice sound natural? Is the playback smooth? Do the controls (play/pause/skip) work as expected? Does the mini player stay visible when you navigate away?

---

### Flow 5: Pantry Scan

**Steps:**
1. Go to the Pantry tab at the bottom
2. Tap the camera icon to scan ingredients
3. Point your camera at items in your fridge or on a receipt
4. Wait for items to be detected and added to your pantry
5. Return to the Feed tab
6. Notice the match percentage badges on recipe cards showing how many ingredients you already have

**What to look for:** Does the scanning work well? Are items detected accurately? Is the match percentage helpful for choosing recipes?

---

### Flow 6: Subscribe to Kindred Pro

**Steps:**
1. Go to the Settings tab
2. Tap on the subscription section
3. Review the Kindred Pro benefits
4. Purchase the subscription (this is in sandbox mode — you won't be charged real money)
5. Notice that ads are removed after purchase
6. Check that you have access to unlimited features

**What to look for:** Is the value proposition clear? Is the purchase flow smooth? Do you notice the difference between free and Pro tiers?

---

## How to Report Issues

The easiest way to send feedback is to **shake your device** while using the app. This will bring up TestFlight's built-in feedback form.

When reporting an issue, please include:
- **What you were doing** (which screen, which button)
- **What happened** (error message, crash, unexpected behavior)
- **What you expected** (what should have happened instead)

Screenshots are super helpful if something looks broken!

---

## Known Limitations

- **Sandbox subscriptions** may behave differently from production subscriptions. You might see test account warnings or different pricing displays.
- **Voice profiles** require uploading audio, which needs microphone or file access permissions. The consent flow explains how your voice data is used.
- **Recipe availability** depends on location and content partnerships. Some areas may have fewer recipes during beta.
