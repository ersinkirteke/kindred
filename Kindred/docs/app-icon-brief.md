# App Icon Design Brief

**App Name:** Kindred
**Category:** Food & Drink
**Platform:** iOS (iPhone)
**Created:** 2026-04-03

---

## Technical Specifications

### Size & Format
- **Dimensions:** 1024x1024 pixels (exact)
- **Format:** PNG (24-bit)
- **Transparency:** None (solid background required)
- **Color mode:** RGB
- **Resolution:** 72 DPI

### iOS Guidelines
- **No rounded corners** — iOS automatically applies corner radius
- **No transparency** — Background must be opaque
- **Safe area:** Keep critical elements 80px from edges (iOS applies 22.5% corner radius)
- **Testing sizes:** Icon will be scaled to 180x180 (iPhone home screen), 120x120 (Settings), 80x80 (Spotlight)

### Output Location
```
Kindred/Sources/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/AppIcon.png
```

## Brand Identity

### App Positioning
Kindred is a cooking app focused on **emotional connection through voice**. The killer feature is hearing a loved one's cloned voice narrate recipes. The icon should evoke:
- Warmth and comfort (home cooking)
- Family and connection (loved ones)
- Modern AI technology (voice cloning)
- Approachability and friendliness

**Core Emotion:** Nostalgia + Innovation

---

## Visual Style Direction

### Style Keywords
- **Warm** — Inviting, comforting, home-like
- **Modern** — Clean lines, contemporary design
- **Friendly** — Approachable, not intimidating
- **Timeless** — Avoid trendy elements that date quickly

### Icon Type
**Symbolic/Illustrative** (not photographic)

Use simplified, stylized imagery that reads clearly at small sizes. Avoid:
- Realistic food photography
- Complex textures or gradients
- Fine details that disappear at small sizes
- Text or letterforms (except subtle monograms if tasteful)

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Warm Cooking Theme)

**Orange Family:**
- `#FF6B35` — Primary orange (vibrant, appetizing)
- `#FF8A5C` — Light orange (warm highlight)
- `#E85D3A` — Red-orange (deeper accent)

**Supporting Colors:**
- `#FF7F56` — Coral (soft warmth)
- `#FFFBF5` — Cream/off-white (contrast, highlights)
- `#F5E6D3` — Warm beige (subtle background if needed)

### Usage
- **Dominant color:** One of the orange/red tones as primary
- **Accent:** Cream or white for contrast and highlights
- **Background:** Can be solid color or subtle gradient (orange to coral)
- **Avoid:** Cool colors (blues, greens) — stay warm to convey cooking/home

## Design Principles

### Readability at Small Sizes
- Icon will appear as small as 80x80px (Settings, Spotlight)
- **Test at 180x180px** (iPhone home screen) — must be instantly recognizable
- Avoid fine lines under 8px width
- Ensure good contrast between elements

### Uniqueness
- Must stand out from other cooking apps (AllRecipes, Tasty, Yummly)
- Differentiate through warm color palette and voice/family theme
- Avoid generic chef hat, fork/knife, or recipe book clichés

### Consistency
- Icon should visually align with app's warm, family-focused brand
- Color palette should match screenshot gradients (orange/coral tones)
- Style should feel modern but not cold/clinical

---

## Examples to Avoid

**Don't:**
- Use realistic food photography (dates quickly, doesn't scale well)
- Include text or app name (unreadable at small sizes)
- Use complex patterns or textures (muddy at 180x180px)
- Copy competitor icons (generic fork/knife, chef hat, recipe card)
- Use cool/tech colors (blue, gray) without warmth
- Over-complicate with multiple elements competing for focus

## Design Process

1. **Brief designer** with this document
2. **Request 2-3 concept sketches** exploring different imagery directions
3. **Select one concept** for refinement
4. **Iterate on color and details** (1-2 revision rounds)
5. **Finalize and export** at 1024x1024px PNG
6. **Test on device** — install in TestFlight and view on home screen
7. **Adjust if needed** — verify readability at small size
